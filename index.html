Rock Paper Scissor

Rock Paper Scissor

is a Classic two-person game.It is a simple and interesting game.

how to play:


There are 10 rounds against computer, who will win more rounds will be the winner!
  1. If you choose Rock,you will win against Scissor but lose against Paper.
  2. If you choose Scissor, you will win against Paper but lose against Rock.
  3. If you choose Paper, you will win against Rock but lose against Scissor.

click on the play button for start the game.

computer (0)

you (0)


click on the hand for choose your turn.
